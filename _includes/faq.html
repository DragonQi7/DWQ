Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dragon's Way Qigong? +

Dragon's Way Qigong is a complete wellness system based on Traditional Chinese Medicine that combines gentle movements, breathing techniques, and energy cultivation to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual balance.

Do I need any prior experience? +

No prior experience is necessary. Our programs are designed for beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Each movement is taught step-by-step with modifications for different abilities.

How long are the sessions? +

Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes, including movement practice, breathing exercises, and brief meditation. We also offer shorter 30-minute sessions for busy schedules.

What should I wear and bring? +

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows for easy movement. Bring a water bottle and an open mind. All other materials will be provided.
