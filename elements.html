The 5 Element Consciousness Framework

The Five Elements—Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water—are the foundation of all life and transformation in Traditional Chinese Medicine. In Dragon's Way Qigong, each movement and teaching harmonizes one or more of these elemental forces.

Wood
Growth, flexibility, creativity
Fire
Passion, joy, expansion
Earth
Nurturing, stability, balance
Metal
Clarity, letting go, refinement
Water
Stillness, wisdom, rene

Together, they form the cosmic dance of energy that flows through all existence.
