About Dragon's Way Qigong®

The Dragon's Way Qigong® system is a classical approach to energy cultivation based on the principles of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM).

Developed by Grand Master Nan Lu, this practice teaches that health and transformation begin from within—when we align with the laws of nature and the rhythm of the universe.

Through gentle yet powerful movements, food therapy, and energy awareness, practitioners learn to release stagnant emotions, awaken their body's natural wisdom, and reconnect to the flow of Qi.

It's not exercise—it's a return to balance.

Dragon’s Way Qigong® is a powerful program designed to bring the body into harmony and restore balance. Rooted in the ancient practice of Wu Ming Qigong and the wisdom of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), it offers a path to true well-being from the inside out.

Additionally, this unique system teaches you how to eat according to your body’s messages and cravings, helping you decode the subtle signs of imbalance with the 5 Element Consciousness Framework before they develop into illness. By realigning with the body’s innate intelligence, you cultivate a foundation of lasting health and strength.

The Five Element Consciousness Framework

The Five Elements—Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water—are the foundation of all life and transformation in Traditional Chinese Medicine. In Dragon's Way Qigong, each movement and teaching harmonizes one or more of these elemental forces.

Wood
Growth, flexibility, creativity
Fire
Passion, joy, expansion
Earth
Nurturing, stability, balance
Metal
Clarity, letting go, refinement
Water
Stillness, wisdom, renewal

Together, they form the cosmic dance of energy that flows through all existence.
